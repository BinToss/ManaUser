New Clothes, as in The Emporor's New Clothes. In other words, this is actually a nude mod. Basically it's Juxtor's Morrowind Nude Mod plus the female Khajiits from Maboroshi Daikon's Full Nude Patch, with a few chnages by me. I recomend this for anyone who, for wantever reason, wants a non-"Better Bodies" nude patch. The Strip or CoverUp bundle below is also included in this download.
Strip or CoverUp
Download Strip Readme CoverUp Readme
Requires: Various
This is a bundle of mods that either undress NPCs or cover them up (in cases where I don't think they were really meant to be naked). See the two readmes above for details. They're designed with the New Clothes mod in mind, but most should work fine with other nude mods.
Undress For Me
Download
Requires: Either Expansion
This turns the <Apparel> option from Companion RPP into "Undress" and make it work on any NPC provided they like you enough. The result is similar to the popular Strip For Me mod, but this lets you choose how much they remove. And instead of dropping their clothes they only take it off. This works with all NPCs (even from mods) and means you can tell them to get dressed again.
